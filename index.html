Jai Hind Vande Mataram • Happy Independence Day

August 15th - India's Independence Day

On August 15, 1947, India achieved independence from British rule, a momentous day marking the culmination of a long struggle for freedom.

The main celebrations are held in New Delhi, with the Prime Minister hoisting the national flag at the Red Fort and addressing the nation.

This day commemorates the sacrifices of countless freedom fighters and the birth of a new, independent India.

Across the country, people celebrate with flag hoisting ceremonies, cultural events, and by remembering the values of freedom and unity.
